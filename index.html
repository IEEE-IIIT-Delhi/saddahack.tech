About

Sadda Hack is a software-cum-hardware hackathon event where developers, students, hobbyists participate to engineer an innovative application revolving around any themes or area of problem they feel passionate about.

FAQs

  • What is the eligibility criteria?

    A team should consist of undergraduates and/or school students from grades 11th/12th. Each team should consist of 4 members.

  • How much is the cost for attending?

    This event is completely free! We’ll provide you with a plethora of resources and tools to hack with.

  • What should I bring to the event?

    You need to carry your college/school ID Card, laptop, phone, chargers, any sleeping equipment you might want and a geeky t-shirt!
